Visited Restaurants

Kasim's Kitchen Overall the food was pretty decent and had selections that we can't find in STL.
Dishes like Mì Quảng and Bò Né, which are some of my favorite dishes that NOT sold in STL!
Felt like the restaurant was like a little hole in the wall that are one of those special spots that only locals know of.
Mia & More "Mia" is translated as Sugar Cane in Vietnamese.
This place sells a variety of Sugar Cane flavored drinks along with refreshing Bubble Tea Drinks!
Loved how they made FRESH-to-order Sugar Cane drinks!
What better way to cool down in the Houston Heat with a cool refreshing, fresh-pressed Sugar Cane drink to quench your thirst!
Pho Sapa A wonderful restaurant that mostly provides different versions of 1 main dish... PHO!
The Pho was absolutely delicious and very aromatic of the herbs.
Definitely reccommend this place if you are a Pho fan!

Overall the Vietnamese Food Scene in Houston,TX did NOT disappoint!
The foods were reasonably priced, and tasted absolutely wonderful.

Overall Thoughts & TIPS

Overall, the Houston Space Center is definitely worth the visit!
It has so many cool attractions and information about Space Exploration.

TIPS: If you ever have a chance to visit the Houston Space Station, some recommendations:

  • Schedule Tram Tour ahead of time prior to arriving to ensure you would have a spot on the day of your visit

  • Expect a FULL day type of fun & excursion, since there are ALOT of fun & interesting things to explore!

  • Try visiting on a normal weekday to avoid crowds, because it was really busy the 4th of July weekend when we visited!

Unfortunately for us, since it was our first time there, we were not aware that the Tram Tour would be sold out for the whole day. And it was super busy & crowded because we visited during 4th of July weekend.

Overall Thoughts & TIPS

Similarly to the Houston Space Center, the Houston Museum of Natural Science is a fun excursion that may take a whole day if you were to explore all the attractions. It's a great place for families to visit with interative activities!

TIPS: If you ever have a chance to visit the Houston Museum of Natural Science, some recommendations:

  • Expect a FULL day of fun & interactive activities that will be interesting things to explore for ALL Ages!

  • Pack a lunch if you don't want to splurge in the Cafe which only sells a limited variety of snacks and sandwiches
  • If you have children, it stroller friendly in certain attractions!
  • Tickets may be required for certain special attractions. And Flash Photography
  • Flash Photography may be prohibited in some Attractions that require tickets.
    But photography and video recording WITHOUT Flash is allowed
